Oxford Icing Wiki

The Oxford Icing Wiki serves as both a knowledgebase for the Icing Group at the Oxford Thermofluids Institute, University of Oxford, and a public resource on icing, covering physical phenomena and numerical methods. The wiki is currently a work in progress.
